Problem : Dandruff

Solution:

Use peppermint or mint-based shampoos. Apply shampoo on scalp, massage in circular motion and then pat on mid hair lenghts.

Home Remedy:

In case of sticky dandruff, hot oil therapy is useful.

  • Heat pure coconut, sesame seed or olive oil.
  • Apply on scalp with cotton wool, rubbing gently to dislodge the flakes.
  • Then dip a towel in hot water, squeeze it and wrap it around your head like a turban.
  • Keep it on for 5 minutes
  • Repeat the hot water towel wrap 3-4 times. This helps the hair and scalp to absorb the oil better.
  • Leave the oil on overnight.
  • Next morning apply the juice of a lemon to your scalp and wash your hair after half an hour
