Problems and Solutions

Problem : Dandruff

Solution:

  • Use peppermint or mint-based shampoos. Apply shampoo on scalp, massage in circular motion and then pat on mid hair lenghts.

Home Remedy:

  • In case of sticky dandruff, hot oil therapy is useful.
  • Heat pure coconut, sesame seed or olive oil.
  • Apply on scalp with cotton wool, rubbing gently to dislodge the flakes.
  • Then dip a towel in hot water, squeeze it and wrap it around your head like a turban.
  • Keep it on for 5 minutes
  • Repeat the hot water towel wrap 3-4 times. This helps the hair and scalp to absorb the oil better.
  • Leave the oil on overnight.
  • Next morning apply the juice of a lemon to your scalp and wash your hair after half an hour

Problem : Breakouts

Solution:

  • Regular care with a cleanser and toner that suit your skin type is essential.
  • The toner should be non-alcoholic and non-astringent.
  • In case of acute acne, avoid any scrubbing as it may spread the infection. Keep your skin as clean as possible.

Home Remedy 1:

  • Apply cucumber water, lemon or fuller's earth powder, but only on the infected areas.

Home Remedy 2:

  • Soak a handful of neem leaves in a litre of water overnight.
  • Next morning, simmer over a low flame till water reduces to half.
  • Cool and strain.
  • Use it to rinse your face
  • Make a paste of the leaves and apply to the affected areas.

Essential Oils

Essential oils have gained popularity for their healing properties that are both beneficial for both beauty and health. Made from the essence of different plants and herbs, these oils are widely used in aromatherapy and naturopathy.

Essential oils are usually concentrated liquids containing volatile aroma compounds from plants.

Essential oils are carrier oils and can't be used directly on the skin. They should only be used with agents like moisturizersm body lotions and hair oils or even water.

More than 3 to 4 drops or these oils may cause irritation and other skin problems. The best part is that essential oils can be used throughout the year irrespective of the season and on all skin types.

Here are some amazing benefits of these oils :

Lavender Oil :

Most commonly used for its relaxing effects on body, lavender oil helps in releiving stress. A drop or two when rubbed on the temples, will help you sleep better. Massaging your hair with a combination of lavender and coconut oil can cure it of lice infestation. Lavendar oil can even relieve period cramps if rubbed on the abdomen. It even restores the complexion of your skin and reduces acne.

Tea-tree Oil :

If you are self-concious about the pimples and pock marks on your face, tea tree oil is the answer to all your skin problems. Use a drop or two of tea tree oil with your regular hair oil to get rid off dandruff and an itchy scalp. Tea tree oil will also help fight psoriasis, zap boils and fungal infections on the feet.

Rosemary Oil :

Extremely beneficial for hair growth, rosemary oil will nourish the hair, making it thick and shiny. It also clears the skin. Apart from its beauty benefits, rosemary oil improves the memory and is beneficial for liver detoxification and gall bladder functions.

Sandalwood Oil :

Rich in antiseptic and antipasmodic properties, sandalwood oil helps cure a host of infections which are common during the monsoons. It helps keep your skin flawless and prevents ageing.

Lemongrass Oil :

The astringent and antimicrobial properties of lemongrass oil help to resolve acne. A facial sauna with a drop or two of lemongrass oil or a facial mask with a hint of this oil will give you fresh and clean skin. Lemongrass oil cures headache, insomania, indigestion

Rose Oil :

This oil acts as an anti-anxiety agent and helps in maintaining hormonal balance. It relaxes the body and relieves stress. Rose oil also has astringent properties that strengthens the gums and hair roots while toning and lifting the skin.

Neroli Oil :

Extracted from bitter orange blossoms, neroli oil is ideal for acne-prone skin. It helps clear wrinkles, scars and stretch marks. Three to four drops of neroli oil in your bath water can relieve fatigue and induce sound sleep. It is natural remedy for anxiety, depression, hysteria, panic, shock and stress.
Essential oils are easily available in the market. Though essential oils are expensive, each drop of these oils is worth a try.
